AEM — Corporate, Product & Patient Support Pages

1. Project Overview

Site: Corporate, Product & Patient Support Websites (Pharmaceutical Client)
Objective: Ensure stable content operations and improve localization quality across global and Japanese sites
Role: Project Lead (Content operations, QA process design, team management)

Led a three-member AEM operations team after the departure of two senior members. Responsible for stabilizing operations, managing delivery, and improving localization quality.

2. Team Structure & Duration

Team size: 3 members (Member → Lead)
Duration: Oct 2017 – Oct 2020

3. Client Challenges & Goals

Inconsistent localization quality and frequent updates caused operational instability. The client required reliable delivery and standardized QA processes.

4. Solutions & Contributions

  • Directed and mentored two junior members, managing tasks and reviewing deliverables
  • Designed and implemented standardized QA and publishing workflows
  • Coordinated with Japanese stakeholders and global teams in English
  • Structured unclear requirements to reduce miscommunication

5. Improvements & Focus Areas

  • Improved Japanese content readability and layout consistency
  • Enhanced UX through page structure and content flow optimization
  • Established clear communication processes across stakeholders

6. Results & KPI

  • Achieved 100% on-time delivery over 3 years
  • Reduced post-release issues by approximately 30%
  • Improved content publishing efficiency by approximately 20%
  • Received recognition for leadership and quality

7. Business Impact

  • Stabilized operations after team restructuring
  • Established scalable workflows applicable to other projects
